Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GramBazaar

How can I become a seller on GramBazaar?

To become a seller, simply sign up for an account, complete your seller profile, and submit your products for verification. Our team will review your application within 24-48 hours.

What types of products can I sell on GramBazaar?

We welcome all agricultural products, handicrafts, handmade items, and traditional rural products. All products must be authentic and locally produced.

How does payment work for sellers?

Sellers receive payments directly to their bank account via UPI within 24 hours of order delivery. We don't hold payments, ensuring you get your money quickly.

What languages does GramBazaar support?

Currently, we support English, Hindi, and Telugu. We're working to add more regional languages in the near future to serve more communities.

How can I get help if I'm facing technical issues?

Our support team is available via phone, email, and chat during business hours. You can also visit our help center for tutorials and troubleshooting guides.
